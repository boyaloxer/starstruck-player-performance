Metric Reference Guide

Adaptability 30%

Measures how quickly the player learns and applies changes—new mechanics, raid plans, or unexpected events mid-encounter. High scores reflect smooth execution after adjustments with minimal reminder.

  • 10: Anticipates adjustments, teaches others, almost zero re-explains.
  • 7–8: Quickly course-corrects after feedback, no repeat mistakes.
  • 4–5: Needs multiple reminders, adapts but slowly.
  • 1–2: Resists changes or repeats mistakes that wipe the raid.

Mechanics Execution 25%

Captures performance on encounter-specific duties—soaks, interrupts, movement, cooldown timing, etc. A high score means reliable execution regardless of pressure.

  • 10: Executes flawlessly, covers for others, never misses assignments.
  • 7–8: Occasional minor slip, recovers without raid impact.
  • 4–5: Misses key mechanics, causes avoidable group damage.
  • 1–2: Frequently dies to basics or causes wipes.

Class Specific Execution 20%

Rates how well the player leverages their toolkit—rotation, cooldown sync, spec choices, utility usage, consumables, and stat priorities. Focus on fundamentals, not just logs.

  • 10: Exemplary use of kit, optimizes for encounter, shares knowledge.
  • 7–8: Strong baseline with room for polish on min-maxing.
  • 4–5: Noticeable gaps (late cooldowns, wrong talents, poor utility).
  • 1–2: Lacks basic understanding, hurts group synergy.

Raid Prep 10%

Reviews readiness outside of combat—consumables, enchants, gear maintenance, boss research, attendance to strategy meetings, and being on time.

  • 10: Always prepared, helps distribute materials or strategies.
  • 7–8: Arrives fully prepped with rare lapses (e.g., one missing flask).
  • 4–5: Frequently forgets consumables or shows up late.
  • 1–2: Chronic lack of prep, others must cover gaps.

Raw Parses 5%

Pure throughput relative to role—DPS, healing, or mitigation. This is the least weighted metric; focus on effort and improvement trajectory, not just color rankings.

  • 10: Competitive logs without sacrificing mechanics.
  • 7–8: Solid numbers, maybe lacking some optimization.
  • 4–5: Underperforms for ilvl/role, inconsistent uptimes.
  • 1–2: Far below peers; indicates rotation or gear issues.

Attitude 5%

Evaluates communication, coachability, and team impact. Scores reflect how the player contributes to raid morale and resolves conflict.

  • 10: Positive force, owns mistakes, uplifts teammates.
  • 7–8: Generally upbeat, accepts feedback, occasional frustration.
  • 4–5: Dismissive of guidance, causes friction, salty comms.
  • 1–2: Toxic behavior, blames others, undermines leadership.

Attendance Policy

Attendance is tracked separately—falling below 80% is an automatic flag for follow-up and may override performance scores. Use the calculator to note the percentage and detail any context in the notes section.
