Metrics Explained

Adaptability 30%

Tracks improvement over time. A high score goes to raiders who absorb feedback, act on it the next pull, and show steady growth from week to week.

  • 10: Returns after feedback with the fix in place, mentors others, no repeats.
  • 7–8: Needs an occasional reminder but corrects mistakes within the session.
  • 4–5: Slow to internalize notes, same errors pop up the next raid night.
  • 1–2: Ignores coaching, repeats critical mistakes, stalls team progress.

Mechanics Execution 25%

Focuses on doing the jobs that keep the raid alive. Killing adds, dispels, kicks, positioning, managing threat, and other tasks that might not be fun but clear content.

  • 10: Flawlessly executes mechanics and prioritizes them above all else.
  • 7–8: Solid execution with occasional misses.
  • 4–5: Late to execute or takes unnecessary damage.
  • 1–2: Ignores mechanics, causes wipes or deaths.

Class Specific Execution 20%

Looks at how the player uses the unique tools their class brings when the raid calls for them: mind controls, banishes, traps, spellsteals, off-heals, totems, cooldown support, and similar class-only jobs.

  • 10: Always ready for class duties, swaps talents or gear for the job, saves pulls with clutch utility.
  • 7–8: Reliable on assigned tasks, may need a reminder for edge cases.
  • 4–5: Hesitates on class abilities or forgets to use them when needed.
  • 1–2: Ignores class toolkit, leaves raid without required utility.

Raid Prep 10%

Reviews readiness outside of combat: consumables, enchants, gear maintenance, boss research, attendance to strategy meetings, and being on time.

  • 10: Always prepared, helps distribute materials or strategies.
  • 7–8: Arrives fully prepped with rare lapses (e.g., one missing flask).
  • 4–5: Frequently forgets consumables or shows up late.
  • 1–2: Chronic lack of prep, others must cover gaps.

Raw Parses 5%

Pure throughput relative to role for DPS, healing, or mitigation. This is the least weighted metric. Focus on effort and improvement trajectory, not just color rankings.

  • 10: Competitive logs without sacrificing mechanics.
  • 7–8: Solid numbers, maybe lacking some optimization.
  • 4–5: Underperforms for ilvl/role, inconsistent uptimes.
  • 1–2: Far below peers; indicates rotation or gear issues.

Attitude 5%

Evaluates communication, coachability, and team impact. Scores reflect how the player contributes to raid morale and resolves conflict.

  • 10: Positive force, owns mistakes, uplifts teammates.
  • 7–8: Generally upbeat, accepts feedback, occasional frustration.
  • 4–5: Dismissive of guidance, causes friction, salty comms.
  • 1–2: Toxic behavior, blames others, undermines leadership.

Attendance Policy

Attendance is tracked separately. Falling below 80% is an automatic flag for follow-up and may override performance scores. Use the calculator to note the percentage and detail any context in the notes section.
